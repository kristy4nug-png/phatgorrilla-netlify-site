Customer information

Shipping & Returns

Phat Gorrilla is a print-on-demand clothing brand. Customers pay by secure GBP checkout powered by Stripe. Products are printed and fulfilled by Printify after purchase.

Shipping

Orders are currently set up for UK checkout. A standard UK delivery charge may be shown at checkout, and final shipping details are confirmed before payment is completed.

Production

Each product is printed after the order is placed. This keeps the range flexible and avoids unnecessary stock waste.

Returns & Issues

If an item arrives damaged, misprinted, defective, or incorrect, contact Phat Gorrilla with your order details and photos so the issue can be reviewed with the fulfilment partner.

Because products are printed on demand, returns for incorrect size choice, change of mind, or customer ordering mistakes may not be accepted unless required by applicable consumer law.
