Size Guide

Phat Gorrilla products are print-on-demand and may use different blanks depending on the product. Check product fit before ordering. If between sizes, consider sizing up for a relaxed streetwear fit.

T-Shirts

Regular tees are usually a classic fit. Oversized and boxy tees are designed to sit wider and looser.

Hoodies

Hoodies are best chosen by chest fit and preferred length. Size up for layering or a heavier streetwear shape.

Sweatshirts

Sweatshirts sit between tee and hoodie fits. Choose your usual size for regular fit, or one size up for relaxed.
