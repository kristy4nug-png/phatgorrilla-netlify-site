# Phat Gorrilla Homepage Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-07

Direction: luxury British streetwear, premium retail quality, clean spacing, strong product focus, black/charcoal with gold and cream accents. Do not copy any existing department store branding, layout, wording, logo, colour ownership, or trade dress.

## Homepage Sections

### 1. Hero

Purpose: make the brand feel premium immediately and send customers to the shop.

Content:

- Brand name: `Phat Gorrilla`
- Headline: `Phat Gorrilla Streetwear`
- Short line: `Premium UK streetwear with heavyweight attitude.`
- Primary button: `Shop Now` -> `/shop/`
- Secondary button: `View Super Elite` -> `/super-elite/` or `/elite/`

Visual direction:

- Dark charcoal or black base.
- Gold accent lines or buttons.
- Cream/white text.
- One strong product or brand visual.
- Avoid clutter and novelty graphics.

### 2. Featured Product Or First Drop

Purpose: show the shop is real and live.

Feature the current product first:

- `Unisex Heavy Cotton Tee`
- Link to `/shop/unisex-heavy-cotton-tee/`
- Show price in GBP.
- Show available sizes/colours if the theme supports it.

Button:

- `View Product`

### 3. Main Shop Intro

Purpose: explain the overall shop range.

Copy:

```text
Bold graphic tees, heavyweight essentials, and statement streetwear pieces built around the Phat Gorrilla identity.
```

Button:

- `Shop the Range` -> `/shop/`

### 4. Super Elite Section

Purpose: make Elite feel more expensive and exclusive.

Visual direction:

- Black, gold, cream, charcoal.
- Minimal layout.
- Fewer words.
- Product-focused.
- More whitespace.

Copy:

```text
Super Elite is the refined side of Phat Gorrilla. Cleaner, darker, sharper, and built around a premium black-and-gold attitude.
```

Button:

- `Enter Super Elite`

## Mobile Rules

- Shop button must be visible without scrolling too far.
- Product image must not be tiny.
- Navigation must be simple.
- Keep text short.
- Avoid stacked clutter.
- Policy and support links must be easy to find.
