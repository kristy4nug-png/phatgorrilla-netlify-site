Phat Gorrilla may receive order, contact, delivery, and app access information needed to support purchases and customer requests. Payments are handled through secure Stripe checkout. Fulfilment is handled by Printify.

Card details are handled by Stripe. Phat Gorrilla does not store card numbers on this website.

Order and delivery details may be shared with Printify and fulfilment partners so products can be printed and shipped.
