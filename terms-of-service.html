About Phat Gorrilla

Phat Gorrilla is an online clothing brand selling premium streetwear, country drops and Super Elite range products through phatgorrilla.com.

Purchases

Customers pay through secure GBP checkout powered by Stripe. Prices, shipping, taxes and totals are shown before payment is completed.

Printing And Fulfilment

Products are printed and fulfilled by Printify and its fulfilment partners after purchase. Product availability, delivery estimates and fulfilment times may vary.

Product Style

Some Phat Gorrilla designs intentionally use rustic, distressed or vintage-style texture as part of the streetwear look. Product colours and placement may vary slightly between mockups and finished items.

App Access

The public app demo is free to view. Full Phat Gorrilla app access is offered after purchase as a brand bonus for updates, drops, previews and launch content.

Returns And Support

For delivery, product or order support, use the contact form on the website. For shipping and returns details, see the Shipping & Returns page.
